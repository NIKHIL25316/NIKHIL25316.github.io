🔍 The Problem

Many digital marketers and business owners spend hours switching between platforms like Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, and spreadsheets — just to get a sense of performance. By the time the insights are compiled, they're already outdated or inconsistent. Manual reporting kills both time and accuracy.

💡 The Opportunity

Instead of juggling between multiple dashboards, imagine a single, unified view that pulls real-time marketing KPIs into one place. That’s exactly what a Marketing Dashboard can do — reduce hours of manual work into just minutes, while giving a birds-eye view of your campaigns.

🩺 Pain Points and Solutions

Scattered Data Across Platforms

Marketers struggle to combine performance data from Google Ads, Meta, LinkedIn, etc. Each platform tells a different story.

Unified Cross-Channel View

Our dashboard brings all channels into one place — with consistent KPIs to compare, optimize, and budget wisely.

Unclear ROI & Attribution

It’s hard to know which platform or campaign is actually driving conversions or revenue — leading to wasted ad spend.

Attribution-Ready Insights

Track metrics like ROAS, assisted conversions, and channel-wise performance — helping you invest in what works.

No Time for Deep Analysis

Marketers spend so much time collecting data, they don’t get to analyze trends or identify underperforming segments.

Insights on Autopilot

With key breakdowns by campaign, audience, and geography, we help you zoom in instantly and act faster.

Static, Outdated Reports

Weekly decks or exported CSVs get outdated quickly, and they don’t offer interactivity for deeper dives.

Interactive Dashboards

Filter by time, campaign, or channel. Drill into what matters — live and always up to date.

⚙️ How I Build It

  • Connect with data sources like Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn, and website analytics tools.
  • Clean, transform, and structure the data using Python and SQL pipelines.
  • Visualize key metrics — ROAS, CTR, Impressions, Conversions — in a clean, interactive dashboard.
  • Automate daily/weekly reporting so your team focuses on strategy, not spreadsheets.
