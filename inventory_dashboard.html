🔍 The Problem

Many businesses struggle to optimize inventory, leading to overstocking, stockouts, and poor demand forecasting. This results in unnecessary costs and lost sales.

💡 The Opportunity

Implementing an inventory management dashboard provides businesses with real-time insights into stock levels, demand trends, and sales forecasts, which helps avoid stockouts and improve operational efficiency.

⚙️ How I Build It

  • Integrate sales, purchase, and inventory data from ERP or internal systems.
  • Clean and structure the data using Python, SQL, or ETL pipelines.
  • Build a dashboard that visualizes stock levels, reorder points, supplier performance, and demand patterns.
  • Set up alerts for low stock levels, excess stock, and high turnover items to automate decision-making.

📈 The Impact

Inventory insights empower businesses to reduce holding costs, improve cash flow, and ensure high product availability — all leading to improved customer satisfaction and profitability.
