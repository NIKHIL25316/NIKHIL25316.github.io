🔍 The Problem

Sales teams struggle with piecing together data from multiple CRM systems, spreadsheets, and databases. The result is inefficient reporting and delayed decision-making that can hurt sales performance.

💡 The Opportunity

A sales analytics dashboard can provide a single, consolidated view of your sales pipeline, performance metrics, and KPIs, empowering sales teams to make faster, data-driven decisions.

⚙️ How I Build It

  • Integrate data from CRM systems, sales tools, and spreadsheets.
  • Use Python and SQL to clean, transform, and structure the data.
  • Visualize metrics like lead conversion, sales funnel, and revenue in a dynamic dashboard.
  • Automate reporting and alert systems to keep teams on track and informed.

📈 The Impact

With a centralized dashboard, sales teams can gain insights into their pipeline at a glance, improve forecasting, and prioritize the right leads, leading to improved sales performance and revenue.
